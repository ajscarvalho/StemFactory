About

In StemFactory, you will design, maintain and manage a stem cell manufacturing facility, making the most of limited resources and turning cures into profit.

Stem cells have very promising therapeutical applications due to their special properties. However, your competitors have several problems that result in providing these therapies at very high costs and to very few people. Can you beat them by producing cheaper cells and making them available to more people in need?

You will start off with a very small facility. From there you will collect profit to invest in new equipments and move up the cell manufacturing ladder.
