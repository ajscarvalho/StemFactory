Problem

Nowadays the previously incurable diseases, are starting to be cured.

Stem cells have very promising therapeutical applications due to their special properties.

Yet there are several problems that result in providing these therapies at very high costs and to very few people.

Solutions

One possible solution is to have a software simulation running, that helps managers make the best decisions possible.

In light of this, a complex yet powerful simulation app was created.

In order to reduce complexity and ease the effort of comprehending the solution, we provide the same simulation software as an ever increasingly complexity game.

The Simulation Game

In Stem Factory, you will design, maintain and manage a stem cell manufacturing facility, making the most of limited resources and turning cures into profit.

You will start off with a very small facility. From there you will collect profit to invest in new equipments and move up the cell manufacturing ladder.
